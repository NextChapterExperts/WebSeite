In a world where data is the new gold, training providers and companies face a critical question: where do our learning materials and user data live? We chose a path that combines **functionality, flexibility and privacy**. Our learning platform is built entirely on **Nextcloud**, hosted with the German specialist [PortKnox](https://www.portknox.net/).

---

## 1. The learner experience: one central hub

Simplicity is key for learners. Instead of jumping between countless apps, our platform offers a single, consistent entry point:

* **Live sessions:** Video built in for interactive exchange — no need for external accounts.
* **Structure with Kanban:** Content is not buried in cryptic folders. **Learning cards on a Kanban board** show progress at a glance; one click opens the material.
* **Mobile first:** Desktop at the office or phone on the train — materials, video channels and calendar stay in sync.

![Platform entry and overview](/blog/nextcloud-lernplattform/01-Entry.png)

## 2. Proven in practice: international reach

The platform has already proven itself in intensive training use and remains stable and accessible worldwide. Recent examples include:

* **Training in Chișinău (Moldova):** Successfully delivered with 35 participants.
* **Training for AgentPark LLC (Armenia):** Intensive learning sessions for 30 participants.

These show that central hosting in Germany and global reach work well together.

![Video session](/blog/nextcloud-lernplattform/02-videoSession.png)

## 3. Functionality: growing with tasks

The platform adapts to skill level. Fine-grained permissions control how people interact:

* **Beginner mode:** Cards are read-only knowledge — ideal for structured onboarding without distraction.
* **Project mode:** For real teamwork, learners use full capabilities: create files, co-edit and share — similar to Microsoft Teams, but in a protected, private environment.
* **No information loss:** Everything stays on the platform — fewer scattered emails and forgotten downloads.

![Kanban boards](/blog/nextcloud-lernplattform/03-Boards.png)

## 4. The admin view: scale through automation

A modern system must be efficient. Behind the scenes we use the powerful **Nextcloud API** to drive the platform with scripts:

* **Automated user management:** Fast provisioning of access.
* **Content deployment:** Filling learning cards and building boards is script-driven — high quality with minimal manual effort.

![Learning cards](/blog/nextcloud-lernplattform/04-LernCards.png)

## 5. Strategic benefits for decision-makers

From a leadership perspective, this architecture offers security and sovereignty:

* **100% GDPR-aligned:** With hosting at [PortKnox](https://www.portknox.net/), data stays under German jurisdiction.
* **Cost efficiency:** Professional hosting plus open-source flexibility is often more economical than expensive proprietary SaaS bundles.
* **Full control:** You own your data and the structure of your learning environment.
